Introduction

The Base 10 system was only decided on because humans have ten fingers. However, a vegisimal system is also possible. Essentially, it is based off of the decimal system, but instead of ending after 9, it makes small edits to the numerals and does one more pass through, as depicted below. For counting on fingers, one simply puts down fingers in the same order they put them up. Thus, a usable vegisimal system that is easily understandable for Base 10 users.
